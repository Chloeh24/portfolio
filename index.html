About

I'm a full-stack web developer based in London.

In my previous life, I worked as a teacher in South Korea and Japan and swapped the chaos of the classroom for the logic of coding and haven't looked back.
Like all coders, I love the challenge of solving a difficult problem but most importantly, I like to keep it simple and focus on creating things which have a positive social impact.

In my down time you can find me curled up with a good book, exploring a new neighbourhood, listening to a podcast - my current favorites are CodeSwitch and the Happiness Lab - or daydreaming about my next travel destination.
